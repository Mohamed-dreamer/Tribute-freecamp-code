Talent without working hard is nothing

Here's a time line of Cristiano Ronaldo life:

  • 1985 - born 5 February in portugal
  • 2002-2003 - started to play for Sporting CP football club At age 16, Ronaldo was promoted from Sporting's youth team by first-team manager László Bölöni, who was impressed with his dribbling. He subsequently became the first player to play for the club's under-16, under-17 and under-18 teams, the B team and the first team, all within a single season"
  • 2003 - Ronaldo's move to Manchester United was completed on 12 August 2003, too late for the 2003 FA Community Shield but in time for their game against Bolton Wanderers on the opening day of the 2003–04 season, and made him the first Portuguese player to sign for the club.[33] His transfer fee made him, at the time, the most expensive teenager in English football history.."
  • 2007-2008 - the quarter-final stage of the 2006–07 UEFA Champions League, Ronaldo scored his first goals in his 30th match in the competition, scoring twice in a 7–1 win over Roma. He subsequently scored four minutes into the first semi-final leg against Milan, which ended in a 3–2 win, but was marked out of the second leg as United lost 3–0 at the San Siro. He also helped United reach the 2007 FA Cup Final, but the final against Chelsea ended in a 1–0 defeat.Ronaldo scored the only goal in the Manchester derby on 5 May (his 50th goal for the club), as United won their first league title in four years.As a result of his performances, he amassed a host of personal awards for the seasone
  • 2008 - Ronaldo became United's first Ballon d'Or winner since Best in 1968,and the first Premier League player to be named the FIFA World Player of the Year..
  • 2009 - transfer to real madrid football club.
  • 2009-2013 - World record transfer and La Liga title
  • 2013-2015 - Consecutive Ballon d'Or wins and La Décima.
  • 2015-2017 -All-time Madrid top scorer
  • 2017-2018 - Fifth Ballon d'Or and fifth Champions League win.
  • 2018 - on 10 July 2018, Ronaldo signed a four-year contract with Italian club Juventus after completing a €100 million transfer, which included an additional €12 million in other fees and solidarity contributions to Ronaldo's youth clubs
  • 2020-2021 - 100 Juve goals, Capocannoniere, and departure
  • 2021–2022 -Return to manchester united football club
  • 2022 - Final season and departure
